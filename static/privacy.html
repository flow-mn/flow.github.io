Privacy Policy for Flow

Effective Date: 10 February, 2024

Introduction:

This Privacy Policy describes how Batmend Ganbaatar, the developer of the Flow app ("App"), handles personal information.

Information We Collect:

The App does not collect any personal information about you. All data, including settings and saved content, is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to us or any third-party.

Use of Information:

As we don't collect any personal information, we have no use for it.

Sharing of Information:

We do not share any personal information with any third-party or organization.

If you use the App on a macOS or iOS operating system, your data may be included in iCloud backups. Please refer to Apple's privacy policies for details on iCloud data management

Security:

We take reasonable steps to protect the security of any information you input into the App, even though it's not personally identifiable. However, as with any app, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to this Policy:

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

Contact Us:

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at flow@gege.mn.
